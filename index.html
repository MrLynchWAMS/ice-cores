Constructing an Argument (CER)

Prompt: Based on the data you collected in your lab notebook, what is the relationship between atmospheric carbon dioxide (CO2) and global temperatures? Does the modern era (Core A) follow the natural historical patterns found in Cores B and C?

Claim

Write a 1-2 sentence statement that answers the prompt.

Evidence

Provide specific numerical data (ppm, °C, Age) from your data table that supports your claim.

Reasoning

Explain *why* your evidence proves your claim. Connect the greenhouse gas data to the temperature changes.
